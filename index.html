CROWDWIRE FEATURES

Check out the features of our System, that will definitely make you use CrowdWire!

EDIT CUSTOM MAPS

Simple and easy to use Map Editor, to make your video-conferencing environment at your Like.

PROXIMITY CHAT

Take your calls to the next level with the Proximity Chat Feature. The closer you get to a Person the easier is to hear and see.

BROADCASTING

Allow some users to speak to the entire audience, useful for conferences, classes, etc.

PRIVATE ROOMS

Create private Rooms in your World, to comunicate privately.

FILE SHARING

Share private files with your friends, co-workers, and with whoever you want.

MASSIVE MEETINGS

Host conferences or classes with a large amount of users.
